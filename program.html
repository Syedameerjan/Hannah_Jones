IELTS
IELTS preparation course overview :

(International English Language Testing System)

Preparation course is designed to equip students with the skills and strategies necessary to succeed in the IELTS exam, whether for Academic or General Training.

The course focuses on all four components of the exam

* Speaking

* Reading

* Writing

* Listening

providing thorough guidance and practice.

Course format:

* In-person / Online Level

* Daily Assessment

* Weekly practice tests, and mock exams

Target audience:

Students planning to take the IELTS exam for academic or immigration purposes. Individuals aiming to improve their English proficiency for study, work, or migration to English-speaking countries.

Course duration:

30 days crash course

(depending on course intensity and student level)
